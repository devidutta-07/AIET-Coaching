Our Mission

Bringing AI & Computer Education to Rural Students in Jajpur, Odisha

We believe that quality tech education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their location or financial background. Our mission is to empower rural students in Jajpur and surrounding areas with practical, industry-relevant skills in AI, automation, and computer science through affordable PGDCA and specialized courses.

Our Vision

Affordable, Modern, Project-Based Training for Everyone

We envision a future where every student in rural Odisha has access to world-class tech education. Through affordable computer courses, hands-on projects, and mentorship, we're building a community of skilled professionals ready to make an impact in the digital world.

Our Story

AIET Coaching Center was founded with a simple belief: technology education should be accessible to everyone. Our founder, recognizing the gap in quality computer training and PGDCA courses in rural areas like Jajpur, started AIET to bridge that divide.

What began as a small initiative has grown into a thriving community of learners, mentors, and professionals. Today, we're proud to have trained hundreds of students in Jajpur and Odisha who are now working in tech companies, pursuing higher education, or starting their own ventures.

We remain committed to our core values: affordability, quality, and accessibility. Every computer course we design, every student we mentor, and every partnership we build is guided by our mission to democratize tech education in rural Odisha.
